Need To Donate

There are many reasons patients need blood. A common misunderstanding about blood usage is that accident victims are the patients who use the most blood. Actually, people needing the most blood include those:
1.Being treated for cancer
2.Undergoing orthopedic surgeries
3.Undergoing cardiovascular surgeries
4.Being treated for inherited blood disorders

Kinds of Blood Group

There are four main blood groups defined by the ABO system:
1.blood group A – has A antigens on the red blood cells with anti-B antibodies in the plasma
2.blood group B – has B antigens with anti-A antibodies in the plasma
3.blood group O – has no antigens, but both anti-A and anti-B antibodies in the plasma
4.blood group AB – has both A and B antigens, but no antibodies
5.Blood group O is the most common blood group. Almost half of the UK population (48%) has blood group O.
Receiving blood from the wrong ABO group can be life threatening.
